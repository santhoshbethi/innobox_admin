Industrial IoT (Construction) Platform

Intelligent Concrete Maturity Monitoring
IoT had started transforming industries, automate processes and improve ROI. In the area of construction, the IoT devices and sensors are collecting job site data in a more affordable, efficient and effective way. IoT has the potential to increase productivity, on site safety and operational efficiency.

Innobox helped one of its customer develop hardware and software system from the concept to deployment. Solution include an IoT device that collects details from concrete and transmit to cloud helping the construction planning engineers to exactly understand the quality and strength of concrete.




A battery based airtight IoT device is designed and developed to withstand the rugged environment of construction. The life of the battery lasts around 2 to 3 months typically so as to suit the construction requirement. The device was built to handle multiple sensors to measure temperature, moisture, pressure, etc,. The firmware needed for the sensors, BLE and micro controllers are handcrafted to suit the needs of IIoT device. Mobile and Cloud applications were developed to process and present the data using Azure cloud platform. Algorithms and Intelligence for the data collected is developed to make decision making easy and improve the productivity and save construction time. This IIoT device is built to be water proof, can sustain 40lbs of pressure and packaged with rugged IP68 enclosure.
