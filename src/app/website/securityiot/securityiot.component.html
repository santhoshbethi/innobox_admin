Security and Surveillance (IIoT)

Object counting and tracking
Security and safety at home, industry and enterprise has always been very important consideration around the world. Advanced IoT solutions are offering this security and safety in the affordable way. With evolution of IoT, there are multiple types of sensors that are designed and developed by different chip manufacturers to address the security and safety aspects.

At Innobox, we have developed and integrated IoT solutions which can determine the object count like people count both indoors and outdoors using mmWave radar sensors. Our strength in understanding the different types of software development kit (SDK) from multiple vendors has helped our clients to come up with quicker solutions. Our team is well versed in bringing out a fast prototype for demonstration of desired solution using chipsets from Texas Instruments, Qualcomm, Expressif etc. Our expertise in understanding the frameworks, interfaces of various SOCâ€™s helped to quickly create customized software, drivers and adaptation of the frameworks to suit the needs of clients.

In one of the IIoT solution, our team had integrated and developed firmware for both mmWave radar sensor and WIFI module. IWR6843 radar sensor from Texas Instruments (TI) and ESP32 WIFI from Expressif has been selected to realize this IoT product. We used the BSP provided by TI and Expressif and customized the drivers to suit the needs. Secured data exchange between devices has been achieved using MQTT messaging protocols.

An application has been developed both on desktop and mobile which interpret the data received from IIoT and represent the same in understandable manner. Data transmission to cloud has been achieved using industry wide popular cloud computing platforms like AWS. Thus offering an end to end development and validation of whole secured IoT solution. This product helped our client to deploy this across multiple areas of health industry as adult care, traffic management and wildlife protection.
