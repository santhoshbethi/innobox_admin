Certification

Ensuring that your product has met industry-agreed requirements of interoperability, security and application-specific protocols to acquire Wi-Fi Alliance Certification requires expert guidance. At Innobox, we help businesses submit their products for testing, earn accreditation with the Wi-Fi Alliance and get certified with zero hassle.

Global Certification Forum (GCF) is mandatory for devices that require wireless connectivity to networks in countries other than North America. GCF certification is applicable to both module and end-product manufacturers; and is issued to products that are in compliance with the cellular network standards of the respective countries.

PCS Type Certification Review Board (PTCRB) is issued for devices that adhere to the standards of interoperability with other mobile devices. Innobox assists organizations in the process of ensuring wireless cellular device quality through rigorous testing to ensure the devices comply with the test requirements as per global industry standards.
