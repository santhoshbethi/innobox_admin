Mobile Apps

Challange

Innobox Inc. has developed a Medical/Healthcare Mobile application that is further connected with the individual doctors and patients applications. The QuiqRx platform enables quick and easy access to, and maintenance of, patient’s electronic health records.

The app also enables doctors to provide remote consultations to patients through seamless report sharing and individual communication features. The platform also enables the clinic staff to create and manage appointments; as well as send appointment reminders to respective patients, thus contributing to the overall efficiency of the clinic operations.

Doctor’s Application:

  • View the entire medical record of the patient at his/her convenience.
  • Issue prescription to remote patients so that he/she can reach out to more patients.
  • Set drug prescription reminders so that patient will never miss medication.
  • Create medication templates for repeated conditions to quickly create a prescription.
  • Collect payments from patients remotely
  • Collect the information regarding important events related to patients.
  • Set the safe levels of different clinical values which patients can use as a reference to improve their health condition
  • Speed up the patient interaction process by assigning cases to several helping hands at his premises (Roaster Doctor, Junior Doctor, Receptionist)
  • Medication allergy alerts to prevent prescribing allergic drug compositions to patients.
  • Record patient's past history for future references.

Patients:

  • Drug Reminders help the patient to take medication at the right time.
  • View/Download Prescriptions at his/her convenience from any place
  • Remote consultation and online report-sharing for doctor’s review
  • Third-party Device integration for error-free collection of glucose and BP values
  • Appointment Alerts to facilitate patients to stay in compliance in chronic diseases.
  • Personalized care with constant communication with doctors and caregivers
  • Patients can pay for the consultations, packages that are offered by several doctors at their convenience.
  • Patients need not to carry the Medical Record or any other records, as all of them are maintained in cloud based environment with access restrictions making the EHR safe and secure.
  • Multi-patient management to manage all the family members by one single doctor.
  • Caretaker system to enable alternate person to view the EHR of the patient and maintain their health condition remotely.
